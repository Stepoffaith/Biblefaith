About US

Pastors Garry Emmanuel Stuurman and Shirley Julia Stuurman. Pastor G. E. Stuurman, is the founder and General Overseer of Bible Faith Ministries SA. He is a Bible Scholar and a renowned International Speaker, Bible Teacher and Evangelist who ministers in different platforms around the world.Through his ministry, people are saved, healed, delivered, we have seen families restored and many miracles have taken place.!

Our Mission

We exist to honor God by establishing Christ-centered, Spirit-empowered, socially responsible churches and ministries in every nation. It gives us great pleasure to welcome you to Bible Faith Ministries SA. BFMSA is a Bible believing Christian Church, with its foundation rooted on the word of God..
